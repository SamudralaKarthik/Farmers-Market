About the Product


Local tomatoes are partly sour and partly sweet and contain many seeds inside which are edible. The red colour present in tomatoes are due to lycopene, an antioxidant. We selectively pick organically grown tomatoes from the best farms.

Benefits


Tomatoes contain lycopene, an antioxidant that reduces the risk of cancer and heart-diseases. They protect eyes from light induced damage.
Essential for pregnant women as these tomatoes protect infants against neural tube defects.
